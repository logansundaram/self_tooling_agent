Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Thank you for shopping with us. This Return & Refund Policy explains the conditions under which products may be returned, exchanged, or refunded. By making a purchase, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Eligibility for Returns

Products may be eligible for return if all of the following conditions are met:

The return request is submitted within 30 calendar days of the delivery date

The item is unused, unwashed, and in original condition

Original packaging, tags, manuals, and accessories are included

Proof of purchase (order confirmation or receipt) is provided

Items that do not meet these criteria may be rejected or subject to partial refunds.

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return or refund:

Final sale or clearance items

Digital products or downloadable software

Gift cards or store credit

Personalized or custom-made items

Items damaged due to misuse, negligence, or improper handling

To initiate a return:

Contact customer support with your order number and reason for return

Receive a Return Authorization (RA) number if approved

Ship the item back using the provided instructions

Returns sent without authorization may not be accepted.
